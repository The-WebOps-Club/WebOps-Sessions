An Introduction To Wordpress
CFI | WebOps Club
Bored of making sites
which just sit on your 
hard drive and don't seem to 
serve any purpose?
Do you want to just get on 
the web with your 
OWN website ASAP?
Use wordpress !
Wordpress is a very powerful
used primarily to create blogs,
but it can also be used to create
stunning webpages!
You want an example?
Yes, our own CFI website
Is completely made in Wordpress
Okay so let's get started
What we need:
Install Wordpress
Find a web host 
(wordpress itself also has hosting)
Getting Started:
We first start with logging into our
wordpress  installation.
Go to http://yoursite/wp-admin
You will see the dashboard.
The dashboard is the place from where you
control your entire site.
On the dash, you will see multiple links:

Dashboard - The dashboard itself
Posts - Here, you can add/edit the posts on
                your "blog"
Media - This page allows you to upload/edit
                  any media (images/video) onto your 
                  website
Links - Making a big website, we can quickly
                lose track of links in our site, which
                can lead to broken links. For this
                purpose we have a "link manager".
Now, try adding a post to your site with
the posts page
Also, go through the themes and pick
out one for your page, you can
change it later, too
Pages - Lists all the pages in your site

Comments - An easy way to keep
of and to control comments on posts
This is all the content part of the
website, but we have still not
explored on how to change
the "look" of our site
Appearance - This is used to apply themes
to your site. Themes can be used to change
the look of your site completely just by a
click of a button.
Plugins - These are used to insert special 
elements, like a carousel(slideshow),
a mutli-level gallery, etc. 
Users, Tools, Settings -
Gen settings for your site

Use a spacebar or arrow keys to navigate
